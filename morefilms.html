More Diverse Culture Films

Besides the three films I've introduced in-depth, there are so many more films about or from diverse cultures! These are some of the ones I didn't get a chance to talk about. I highly recommend checking these out if any of these movies or cultures interest you. While Hollywood films are the entertainment that we mainly consume, it's important to remember there's so much more beyond Hollywood. And often those films teach you way more than what you expect.

Wild Tales (2014)

An Argentine-Spanish film that consists of 6 short films, all about revenge and vengeance

The Host (2006)

A South Korean film about a family fighting a mysterious monster who kidnapped their family member

Crazy Rich Asians (2018)

An American film about a Chinese-American professor who travels to Singapore to meet her rich boyfriend

Cinema Paradiso (1988)

An Italian film about an Italian filmmaker who recalls his childhood memories of films, hometown, and friends
